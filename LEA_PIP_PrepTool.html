LEA prep Self-check before the conversation
L — Listen
What outcome are you hoping for this employee? Resist the urge to explain first — your goal in the first 10 minutes is to hear their perspective.
E — Empathize
What assumptions are you carrying in? What might you be missing about their situation? Empathy does not mean agreement — it means they feel heard.
A — Ask
What curious questions will you ask to uncover root causes — not build a case? Try: "What's getting in the way for you?" or "What would success look like from your side?"
